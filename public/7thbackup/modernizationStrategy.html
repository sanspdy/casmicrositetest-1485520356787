Analytics based roadmap for self-funded modernization

Unlock today’s savings to pay for tomorrow’s dreams …

Managing the ecosystem of applications is becoming more challenging as companies digitally transform from legacy systems to more dynamic, user-centric experiences - from social to mobile, Internet of things to the API economy.

Are you able to adapt quickly enough given the complexity of applications, technical obsolescence, velocity of change, and market forces? Do you know how to achieve your vision?

Application Modernization brings together IBM’s expertise in state-of-the-art architectures and development, Design Thinking, rapid modernization of existing systems and data, and DevOps to plan and deliver the near-term and strategic changes required to meetModernization Strategy is a systematic approach to continuously modernize and optimize application and technology portfolios. It uses a set of proven patterns and analytics to rapidly identify actionable opportunities that can save 30-40% on applications and infrastructure – savings that can be used to fund modernization efforts.

Client benefits:

• Leverage cloud for faster time-to-market and lower cost of ownership.
• Enable operating model simplification.
• Open up legacy applications to new digital channels, sustain key legacy applications, and improve resilience.
