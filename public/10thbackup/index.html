Cloud adoption

Whether you are looking to make a few tactical changes to your infrastructure based on cost imperatives or want to enable cloud-based transformation for strategic growth and competitive advantage, we can provide the end-to-end consulting and delivery services to meet the specific cloud adoption objectives of your business. IBM can help design, plan and execute the optimal plan for cloud adoption in your enterprise, regardless of what your drivers to move to the cloud are.

Hybrid IT cloud adoption journey

A typical path in the hybrid IT cloud adoption journey may begin by understanding your overall server and application portfolio—and
progress from rationalizing the elements that need to be migrated, planning and strategizing your migration, and finally, deploying
applications on the cloud. But no two cloud journeys are the same—your entry in the hybrid IT lifecycle can be at any stage: discovery,
rationalization, planning and strategy or execution and implementation.

Client Journey

You can commence your journey to applications or enterprise IT cloud across different entry points. But no matter where you start your
as-a-service transformation, a clear set of approaches defined according to the business case, maturity and nature of your apps, cloud
migration objectives and overall business value is crucial.
